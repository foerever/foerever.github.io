About Me

Hi! My name is Sung Soo (Anthony) Cho. I’m currently a software engineer on the Core Domain Services team at Included Health (formerly Grand Rounds Health), a healthtech startup in San Francisco. Before this, I graduated with a BS in Computer Science with a specialization in high performance data platforms from Rice University class of 2020.

While at Rice I was most involved with the Rice CS Club and Rice Entrepreneurship Community. I served as President, External Vice President, Treasurer, HackRice 6 & 7 organizer, and Freshman Representative of Rice CS Club. In the Rice entrepreneurship space, my partner and I have won 11,000 USD from the LILIE New Entrepreneurs Grant and Rice Undergraduate Elevator Pitch Competition.

Beyond these two communities, I was also involved academically as a teaching assistant (COMP 321 computer systems & COMP 430 database systems) and as a part of the PlinyCompute research group. During the breaks, I’ve enjoyed SWE internships at Cisco Meraki, United Airlines, ParkIT, Sensorfield, and the Center for Space Research (NASA grant). In my spare time, I love to work on side projects and participate in hackathons.
